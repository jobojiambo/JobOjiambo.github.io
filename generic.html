Regional Inequality Outlook

In this project I analyze Inequalty relative to Income, Happiness and Satisfaction.
The objective is to come up with National, Regional, and Global Outlook
Skills: Python, SQL, Tableau, Statistics, and Clustering

The concept of global inequality emanates from the disparities in the distribution of resources among different regions, among world citizens and even between the countries.
For effective analysis, I did data cleaning and preparation using PYTHON. Using Pandas, Numpy and a variety of other Python Libraries, I was able to read, manipulate and generate statistical inferences like Mean, Standard Deviation, and Variances.
I did further data cleaning in SQL where I generated some extra metrics for visualization in Tableau. (For Tableau Click on Image)
